About

I am a third-year Ph.D. candidate at UCLA, working on data-driven methods in civil engineering under the supervision of Professor Ertugrul Taciroglu.

My goal is to use AI-powered techniques to address real-world problems such as natural disasters and the challenges they put on the community. I am increasingly enthusiastic about AI for Good and cannot wait to see how it will enhance our understanding of the Earth and the universe.
